Space Fight Jam:

Solar Shield: UFO

About:
A Spacefighter game about taking down UFOs within Earth and the Moon’s gravity well.

Synopsis: 
You have been selected by the shadowy Solar Shield program of the global shadow government to fly the TR-48 “Dulce”. Do what you must to prevent unauthorized xenos from pillaging the populace you and your fellow pilots must prevent that form happening. 

Features:
Traditional space fighter gameplay
Power Management 
Fire energy weapons and missiles
AI controlled spaceships friend and foe.
2 Levels
